HITAM Clubs & Societies

Affinity Clubs

HITAM encourages students to explore their talents and interests through clubs such as Literary Intellects, Photography, Yoga, Robotics, Cultural, Sports and more. These clubs organize events and competitions to foster creativity and collaboration.

Visit Affinity Clubs on Instagram

Technical Clubs

These include AI-ML Club, IoT Club, Coding Club, Data Science Club, and more. They provide platforms for students to work on live projects, hackathons, and skill development with guidance from faculty and industry mentors.

Literary and Cultural Clubs

The Literary Club hones language and communication skills through debates, creative writing, and book readings. Cultural Clubs promote dance, music, drama, and fine arts through events, workshops, and annual festivals.

Sports Club

The Sports Club actively engages students in physical activities like cricket, basketball, volleyball, table tennis, and athletics. HITAM students have represented at university, state, and national levels.

Visit Sports Club on Instagram

Student Self Governance (SSG)

SSG at HITAM empowers students by involving them in decision-making, event organization, and feedback management. It fosters leadership, responsibility, and collaboration between students and management.

Visit SSG on Instagram

Social Clubs

These clubs promote social impact through awareness campaigns, blood donation drives, Swachh Bharat activities, and more. They aim to build socially responsible and empathetic citizens.

Innovation and Entrepreneurship Cell (IIC)

The IIC supports startup ideas and entrepreneurship initiatives. Students get mentorship, incubation support, and opportunities to pitch to investors and showcase at expos.
