Affinity Clubs

Our clubs cater to a wide range of passions, from the Literary Intellects Club and Cultural Club to the Photography Club and Yoga Club. These student-driven organizations host events, workshops, and competitions, enriching the campus experience beyond academics.

STUDENT SELF GOVERNANCE (SSG)

SSG at HITAM empowers students to take on leadership roles, bridging the gap between the administration and the student body. By participating in management review meetings, students enhance their leadership skills and contribute to institutional decision-making.

NATIONAL CADET CORPS

Established in 2022, our NCC Army Wing comprises 36 cadets who actively engage in various activities, promoting discipline and a sense of duty. The NCC unit leads initiatives for social awareness and community service, instilling patriotism and leadership qualities among students.

What Sets HITAM Apart

HITAM stands out with its commitment to innovation, hands-on learning, and sustainability. Our curriculum is designed to empower students through real-world projects, dedicated faculty, and a focus on ethical leadership. With industry tie-ups, state-of-the-art labs, and a vibrant campus life, HITAM nurtures both professional and personal growth.
